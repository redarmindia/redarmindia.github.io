About REDARM

REDARM (Research, Engineer & Develop the AI & Robotics for Mobility)


We are an early-stage startup in the field of autonomous vehicles, working on a portable solution for vehicle tele-operation & autonomy.

Many attempts by several companies proved that full-autonomy (SAE level-5) is several years ahead. Our approach initially includes a human in the control loop through remote-driving (a.k.a. tele-operation).

We are a group of passionate and seasoned automotive engineers with experience ranging from embedded systems, instrumentation & measurements, power electronics, wireless-communications, vehicle dynamics, simulations & optimization, electromagnetics, control systems, and vehicle testing among others.

We conceptualized (and are working on protos & demonstrations for) tele-operation in multiple geographies. We are working on novel concepts and engineering details for the tele-operation and vehicle actuation, along with some patent applications.

  • Our Story 2021

    This is the year of planning and execution. We started working on the plan for MVPs along the way to the main deliverable.

  • Our Story 2020

    The concept evolved into the driving robot. Using the additional time-at-hand during the world-wide lock-downs, this pitch went through multiple (CXO level) people and it was well received.

    Several ideas for patents were conceived and checked against the massive database of paetnts across the globe.

    The proposal suggested several branching business cases, each of which could be a viable business.

  • Our Story 2019

    The concept for REDARM originated from our founder's experience with the taxi-drivers during his family trip in India. With the understanding that full-autonomy for the vehicles is a distant dream, he looked at tele-operation as a quick solution that can pave way for the future of vehicle autonomy.

About the logo


Our logo symbolizes the Human+Machine intelligence working on providing a way for (future) vehicle mobility.

The downward red-triangle in the background represents the 'give way' or 'yield' sign standardized by the Vienna Convention of Road Signs and Signals.

The envelope of two colored shapes is a circle (hinting mobility). The two halves resemble the shape of brain (hinting intelligence for mobility). The left half is in red (hinting emotion/human intelligence). The right half is in blue (hinting logic/machine intelligence).

About the logo-colors


Our logo has two colors, Red-Orange and Royal-Blue. White is imperative.

Red-Orange (#ff5349) is a cross between the two hues. More red than orange, it is bright and exciting and associated with physical energy. It is playful, energetic, and engaging. Red orange can also symbolize health and vitality.

Royal-Blue (#4169e1) is an eye-catching, versatile color. Blue is associated with trustworthiness and reliability. Common association for blue includes peacefulness. Blue is the color of the sky and the ocean, both of which are said to promote feelings of tranquility..
